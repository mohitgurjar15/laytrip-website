Why Laytrip?

Laytrip is an emerging online travel agency that strives to provide an easy, inspiring and affordable platform for all. To all the dreamers who await new adventures, to all the doers who are already packing their bags with passion and thrill and to all the travellers who are always on the go - Laytrip is the perfect getaway for the next best destination!

Below are the ways we want you to enjoy while using Laytrip:


Afford your travel

We want to help you easily afford your trip. Our buy now pay later option is centered around providing the most efficient way of paying for travelling at the cheapest price. We work hard to determine the most efficient dates for you to book your travel and make suggestions to you in real time.
Read more about our partial payments plan


Get inspired

With our community of travellers and TripFluencers you can easily explore popular destinations all around the world and get insights about particular cities. Browse through thousands of reviews from certified tripfluencers, read their blogs and check-out pictures to get inspired to book your next trip!
Check-out our explore page


Inspire others

Become a TripFluencer and share your experiences and thoughts with the public while travelling with Laytrip. Tripluencers not only inspire the community of travellers but also get more benefits.
Learn more about tripfluencers


Earn points and get discounts

With every friend that signs up with your personal URL - you earn laytrip points which accumulate on your account and can be used to reduce your booking total, make new bookings, or give laytrip points as gifts. If you want more out of your points, become a subscribed user and track all the referral points you earn through your friends and friends of friends. Another option, become a certified TripFluencer! Go on trips though Laytrip and share your experiences with your social media audience. You will earn more points if you are a TripFluencer and will have access to some of our exclusive features.
